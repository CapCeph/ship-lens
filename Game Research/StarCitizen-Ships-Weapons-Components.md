## Weapon Categories & Turret Types

### Weapon Placement Categories

| Category | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **Pilot-Controlled** | Fixed or gimballed weapons controlled directly by the pilot |
| **Manned Turrets** | Physical turrets requiring a player/NPC to sit inside; become breach points when destroyed |
| **Remote Turrets** | Controlled from a station elsewhere on the ship; no physical entry point; often smaller weapons |
| **Point Defense Cannons (PDC)** | AI-controlled turrets that auto-target missiles/torpedoes (e.g., M2C "Swarm") |
| **AI/Blade-Controlled** | Any turret can be AI-controlled with a computer "blade" installed |

### Weapon Types

| Type | Damage Type | Characteristics |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Laser Repeaters** | Energy | High fire rate, consistent DPS, no ammo |
| **Laser Cannons** | Energy | High alpha damage, slower fire rate |
| **Ballistic Repeaters/Gatlings** | Physical | High fire rate, uses ammo, armor penetration |
| **Ballistic Cannons** | Physical | High alpha damage, uses ammo |
| **Distortion** | Distortion | Disables power systems, no hull damage |
| **Neutron Weapons** | Energy | Very high damage, short range |
| **Mass Drivers** | Physical | High velocity projectiles, uses ammo |
| **Scatterguns** | Various | Shotgun-style spread, close range |

## 4.5 Changes Summary

- **170+ weapon changes** with significant health buffs (CF Repeaters +400% HP)
- **Massively increased ammo capacities** (laser capacitors, ballistic magazines)
- **New engineering gameplay** - power distribution, component damage, fuses
- **Armor penetration** system makes ammo/weapon choice more tactical
- **PDCs now functional** for missile/torpedo interception
- **Component degradation** - damaged components perform proportionally worse
- **Power distribution controls** for Shields, Weapons, Thrusters, Life Support
